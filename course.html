Course Outline

This page explains the structure of the Year 11 Art or Product Design course and how projects are created.

Term 1 - 1.1 Significant Place

Explore the concept of a significant location through painting and printmaking techniques.

Term 2 - 1.2 Resolved Artwork

Develop a fully resolved artwork based on previous studies and techniques learned in Term 1.

Term 3 - 1.4 Folio Board (External)

Prepare your external folio board, showing the development of ideas and final resolved works.

Term 4 - Senior School Art Competition

Produce and present work for the annual Senior School Art Competition.
